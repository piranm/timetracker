Working Hours

What are your normal working hours?

The time grid shows a lighter grey for your working hours. Looking at the summary row makes it clearer when you've not worked normal hours.


Display Hours

When the time grid is displayed, what hours do you want shown on it?

If you sometimes come in early or work late, then setting this outside your normal working hours makes it quicker to record time there.

You can always use the [<] and [>] buttons to show more hours.
