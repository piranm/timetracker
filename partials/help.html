Time Tracker provides a quick and simple way to record your time. Usually it just takes one click, and logs your time in your browser.

The Time Grid

For each day, the site presents a grid for the day. Like the one shown below:

Inside the time grid, click once to insert ◉ for a full half-hour.

Clicking again turns the mark into a ◐ for quarter-of-an-hour. Clicking again removes the mark.

The 'Summary' line shows a green ◉ or ◐ if there's time recorded above. If you record more than half-an-hour, then a red ◈ is shown.

A set of tasks are on the left; you can write anything here you want. You can add more tasks by clicing the [+] button. You can delete tasks with the [X] butons.

The Yellow line shows the current time; the grey background shows your normal working hours. You can show earlier hours by clicking [<] or later hours by clicking [>] -- see the Settings to change your normal working horus.

Days and Weeks

The Day Comment allows you to write something memorable about the day, like "Xmas Party" or "Vacation".

Days are grouped into weeks, showing he ISO week number.

The [+] and [-] buttons show or hide the details of a day.

Week and Month Navigation

At the top of the window are three controls over the weeks displayed:

Clicking [<] shows another week back in time; clicking [<<] shows the whole month.

To hide all the weeks apart from the current one, click [X].

A new week will appear automatically at midnight on Monday.

Notifications

Use the Settings button to enable notifications:

The notification window appars when you've got time to fill in. Clicking inside the notification brings the Time Tracker to the front.

(you must keep the Time Tracker website open for notifications to work)

Where's my data stored?

The information you enter is stored on your computer, and not sent over the internet at all.

However, you must be careful when deleting your browser history. Time Tracker uses 'localStorage' and that can be deleted if you're not careful. Always remember to back up your computer!

The Export tab allows you to export your data to a spreadsheet.
